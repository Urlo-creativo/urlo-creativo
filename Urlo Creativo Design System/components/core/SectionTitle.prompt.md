The house section headline — huge, bold, ALL-CAPS, with an optional **italic accent word**.

```jsx
<SectionTitle>PROJECTS</SectionTitle>
<SectionTitle accent="POTENTIAL">Every brand has a</SectionTitle>
<SectionTitle accent="CLIENTS">Selected</SectionTitle>
<SectionTitle size={64} accent="WORK" underline>How we</SectionTitle>
```

Children are uppercased automatically. Keep to one accent word. Use `size` 36–96 depending on hierarchy.
