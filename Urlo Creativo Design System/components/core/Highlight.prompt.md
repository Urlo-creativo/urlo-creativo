The marker-pen accent — the one playful move in the system. Use **sparingly**, on a single word or short phrase.

```jsx
<h2>HOW WE <Highlight mode="underline">WORK</Highlight></h2>
<p>We <Highlight mode="text" color="var(--uc-blue)">identify</Highlight> the brand and define its <Highlight mode="text" color="var(--uc-pink)">personality.</Highlight></p>
<span><Highlight>WE DEVELOP IDEAS THROUGH WORDS AND IMAGES.</Highlight></span>
```

Modes: `fill` (yellow block), `underline` (yellow bar), `text` (coloured block, pass a process accent). Never highlight more than a phrase.
